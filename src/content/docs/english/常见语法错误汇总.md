## 1. 主谓一致错误 (Subject-Verb Agreement Errors)

### 1.1 集合名词的主谓一致

| 错误                                     | 正确                                      | 解析                    |
| ---------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ----------------------- |
| The committee **are** meeting today.     | The committee **is** meeting today.       | committee 作为整体→单数 |
| The committee **is** divided in opinion. | The committee **are** divided in opinion. | 强调个体成员→复数       |

**规则：** 集合名词（family, team, committee, audience）视作整体时用单数，视作个体时用复数。

### 1.2 就近原则

| 错误                              | 正确                             | 解析                         |
| --------------------------------- | -------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Neither he nor I **is** wrong.    | Neither he nor I **am** wrong.   | 就近原则：谓语与最近主语一致 |
| Either you or she **have** to go. | Either you or she **has** to go. | 就近原则：she → has          |

### 1.3 特殊主语

| 错误                                   | 正确                                  | 解析                      |
| -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- | ------------------------- |
| Each of the students **have** a book.  | Each of the students **has** a book.  | each + 单数谓语           |
| The number of errors **are** small.    | The number of errors **is** small.    | the number of → 单数      |
| A number of students **is** absent.    | A number of students **are** absent.  | a number of = many → 复数 |
| Two plus two **are** four.             | Two plus two **is** four.             | 运算结果→单数             |
| Bread and butter **are** my breakfast. | Bread and butter **is** my breakfast. | 视为一个整体→单数         |

## 2. 时态错误 (Tense Errors)

### 2.1 时态不一致

| 错误                               | 正确                               | 解析                   |
| ---------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- | ---------------------- |
| She said she **is** tired.         | She said she **was** tired.        | 主句过去时→从句过去时  |
| I **have seen** him yesterday.     | I **saw** him yesterday.           | yesterday → 一般过去时 |
| She **is living** here since 2020. | She **has lived** here since 2020. | since → 现在完成时     |

### 2.2 完成时误用

| 错误                                         | 正确                                              | 解析                        |
| -------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| I **have been knowing** him for years.       | I **have known** him for years.                   | know 是状态动词，不用进行时 |
| She **has gone** to Paris last week.         | She **went** to Paris last week.                  | 具体过去时间 → 一般过去时   |
| I **have written** the report for two hours. | I **have been writing** the report for two hours. | 强调持续过程 → 完成进行时   |

### 2.3 主将从现

| 错误                                    | 正确                               | 解析                        |
| --------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| I will call you when I **will arrive**. | I will call you when I **arrive**. | 时间/条件从句用现在时表将来 |
| If it **will rain**, I'll stay home.    | If it **rains**, I'll stay home.   | 条件从句用现在时表将来      |

## 3. 冠词错误 (Article Errors)

### 3.1 不定冠词 a/an

| 错误                              | 正确                             | 解析                          |
| --------------------------------- | -------------------------------- | ----------------------------- |
| He is **a** honest man.           | He is **an** honest man.         | honest 首音素为元音 /ɒ/       |
| She is **an** university student. | She is **a** university student. | university 首音素为辅音 /juː/ |
| I saw **a** one-legged man.       | I saw **a** one-legged man.      | one 首音素为辅音 /w/          |

### 3.2 定冠词 the

| 错误                       | 正确               | 解析                   |
| -------------------------- | ------------------ | ---------------------- |
| I love **the** music.      | I love music.      | 泛指不可数名词不加 the |
| She plays **the** tennis.  | She plays tennis.  | 球类运动不加 the       |
| He goes to **the** school. | He goes to school. | go to school = 去上学  |
| **The** life is beautiful. | Life is beautiful. | 抽象名词泛指不加 the   |

### 3.3 零冠词

| 错误                                   | 正确                         | 解析               |
| -------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ------------------ |
| She is **a** teacher and **a** writer. | She is a teacher and writer. | 一个人兼任两个身份 |
| I have **a** breakfast at 7.           | I have breakfast at 7.       | 三餐前不加冠词     |
| He is in **the** hospital.             | He is in hospital. (英式)    | in hospital = 住院 |

## 4. 介词错误 (Preposition Errors)

### 4.1 固定搭配

| 错误                                  | 正确                                     | 解析                     |
| ------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| She is good **in** math.              | She is good **at** math.                 | be good at               |
| He arrived **to** the airport.        | He arrived **at** the airport.           | arrive at/in             |
| She depends **of** him.               | She depends **on** him.                  | depend on                |
| I'm interested **about** AI.          | I'm interested **in** AI.                | be interested in         |
| She married **with** him.             | She married him.                         | marry 是及物动词         |
| He congratulated **him** his success. | He congratulated him **on** his success. | congratulate sb. on sth. |

### 4.2 时间介词

| 错误               | 正确               | 解析          |
| ------------------ | ------------------ | ------------- |
| **In** Monday      | **On** Monday      | 具体某天用 on |
| **In** 9 o'clock   | **At** 9 o'clock   | 具体时刻用 at |
| **On** June        | **In** June        | 月份用 in     |
| **In** the weekend | **On** the weekend | 周末用 on     |

## 5. 代词错误 (Pronoun Errors)

### 5.1 指代不清

| 错误                                       | 正确                                               | 解析              |
| ------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| When she met Mary, she told her the news.  | When she met Mary, **Jane** told her the news.     | 两个 she 指代不明 |
| He told his brother that **he** was wrong. | He told his brother that **he himself** was wrong. | he 指代不明       |

### 5.2 格的错误

| 错误                              | 正确                            | 解析                                      |
| --------------------------------- | ------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| **Me** and him went to the store. | **He and I** went to the store. | 主格作主语                                |
| She is taller than **me**.        | She is taller than **I**.       | than 是连词，后接主格（口语中 me 可接受） |
| Between you and **I**...          | Between you and **me**...       | 介词后用宾格                              |

### 5.3 不定代词

| 错误                                    | 正确                                     | 解析            |
| --------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | --------------- |
| Everyone have **their** own opinion.    | Everyone has **his or her** own opinion. | everyone → 单数 |
| Neither of the answers **are** correct. | Neither of the answers **is** correct.   | neither → 单数  |

## 6. 从句错误 (Clause Errors)

### 6.1 定语从句

| 错误                                         | 正确                                      | 解析                  |
| -------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| The book **what** I bought is good.          | The book **that/which** I bought is good. | what 不能引导定语从句 |
| This is the house **where** he lives **in**. | This is the house **where** he lives.     | where 已含 in 之意    |
| The man **which** called you is here.        | The man **who** called you is here.       | 先行词是人 → who      |

### 6.2 名词性从句

| 错误                                   | 正确                                    | 解析                         |
| -------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| **If** she will come is uncertain.     | **Whether** she will come is uncertain. | 主语从句用 whether 不用 if   |
| I don't know **that** to do.           | I don't know **what** to do.            | that 不作成分，what 作宾语   |
| The reason is **because** she was ill. | The reason is **that** she was ill.     | 表语从句用 that 不用 because |

### 6.3 状语从句

| 错误                                               | 正确                                       | 解析                     |
| -------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------ | ------------------------ |
| **Although** she was tired, **but** she continued. | **Although** she was tired, she continued. | although 和 but 不能连用 |
| **Because** she was ill, **so** she stayed home.   | **Because** she was ill, she stayed home.  | because 和 so 不能连用   |

## 7. 非谓语动词错误 (Non-finite Verb Errors)

### 7.1 不定式 vs. 动名词

| 错误                                                   | 正确                                                        | 解析                                                 |
| ------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| She enjoys **to read**.                                | She enjoys **reading**.                                     | enjoy + doing                                        |
| She stopped **smoking** (= she was a smoker and quit). | She stopped **to smoke** (= she stopped in order to smoke). | stop doing vs. stop to do                            |
| I remember **to meet** him before.                     | I remember **meeting** him before.                          | remember doing = 记得做过；remember to do = 记得要做 |
| She suggested **to go** home.                          | She suggested **going** home.                               | suggest + doing                                      |

### 7.2 分词错误

| 错误                                                     | 正确                                            | 解析                           |
| -------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| **Seeing** from the hill, the city is beautiful.         | **Seen** from the hill, the city is beautiful.  | 城市被看 → 过去分词            |
| **Born** in a rich family, his parents sent him to Eton. | **Born** in a rich family, he was sent to Eton. | 分词逻辑主语必须与主句主语一致 |

### 7.3 常见搭配

| 动词 + to do                            | 动词 + doing                                  |
| --------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| want, hope, decide, agree, refuse, plan | enjoy, avoid, mind, finish, practice, suggest |
| promise, expect, pretend, offer, fail   | consider, deny, risk, imagine, keep           |
| afford, manage, tend, choose, demand    | appreciate, escape, miss, resist, involve     |

## 8. 虚拟语气错误 (Subjunctive Mood Errors)

### 8.1 if 条件句

| 错误                                            | 正确                                     | 解析                   |
| ----------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | ---------------------- |
| If I **was** you, I would go.                   | If I **were** you, I would go.           | 虚拟语气用 were        |
| If I **would have known**, I would have helped. | If I **had known**, I would have helped. | 与过去相反：had + done |
| If she **has** time, she would help.            | If she **had** time, she would help.     | 与现在相反：过去式     |

### 8.2 名词性从句中的虚拟

| 错误                                      | 正确                                      | 解析                            |
| ----------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| She suggested that he **goes** home.      | She suggested that he **go** home.        | suggest + (should) + 动词原形   |
| It is essential that she **is** informed. | It is essential that she **be** informed. | essential + (should) + 动词原形 |

**需用虚拟语气的动词/形容词：**

- 动词：suggest, recommend, require, demand, insist, order, propose
- 形容词：essential, important, necessary, vital, crucial, imperative

## 9. 比较级错误 (Comparison Errors)

### 9.1 比较级形式

| 错误                                              | 正确                                                    | 解析                  |
| ------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------- |
| She is **more better** than him.                  | She is **better** than him.                             | better 本身已是比较级 |
| She is **taller** than **any girl** in the class. | She is **taller** than **any other girl** in the class. | 排除自身              |
| She is **the tallest** of **the two**.            | She is **the taller** of **the two**.                   | 两者之间用比较级      |

### 9.2 比较对象一致

| 错误                                              | 正确                                                      | 解析             |
| ------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------- |
| The population of China is larger than **India**. | The population of China is larger than **that of India**. | 比较对象必须一致 |
| His code is cleaner than **her**.                 | His code is cleaner than **hers**.                        | 名词性物主代词   |

## 10. 标点与格式错误 (Punctuation Errors)

### 10.1 逗号拼接 (Comma Splice)

| 错误                                | 正确                                   | 解析                         |
| ----------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| She was tired**,** she went to bed. | She was tired**.** She went to bed.    | 两个独立句子不能仅用逗号连接 |
| She was tired**,** she went to bed. | She was tired**, so** she went to bed. | 加连词                       |
| She was tired**,** she went to bed. | She was tired**;** she went to bed.    | 用分号                       |

### 10.2 撇号错误

| 错误                       | 正确                      | 解析                     |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| **it's** tail              | **its** tail              | its = 它的；it's = it is |
| **The dog's** are playing. | **The dogs** are playing. | 复数不加 's              |
| **CD's** for sale          | **CDs** for sale          | 缩写词复数不加撇号       |

## 11. 易混淆词汇 (Commonly Confused Words)

| 易混淆词                | 区别                                      | 示例                                                              |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------- |
| affect / effect         | affect = v. 影响；effect = n. 效果        | The rain **affected** the game. / The **effect** was significant. |
| lie / lay               | lie = 躺；lay = 放置                      | She **lies** on the bed. / She **lays** the book down.            |
| rise / raise            | rise = 不及物上升；raise = 及物举起       | Prices **rise**. / She **raises** her hand.                       |
| sit / set               | sit = 坐；set = 放置                      | She **sits** down. / She **sets** the table.                      |
| who / whom              | who = 主格；whom = 宾格                   | **Who** called? / **Whom** did you call?                          |
| fewer / less            | fewer + 可数；less + 不可数               | **fewer** errors / **less** time                                  |
| between / among         | between 两者之间；among 三者以上          | **between** you and me / **among** the students                   |
| principal / principle   | principal = 主要的/校长；principle = 原则 | the **principal** reason / a matter of **principle**              |
| stationary / stationery | stationary = 静止的；stationery = 文具    | a **stationary** object / office **stationery**                   |
| complement / compliment | complement = 补充；compliment = 赞美      | Wine **complements** the meal. / She received a **compliment**.   |
