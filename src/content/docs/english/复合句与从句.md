## 1. 复合句概述

复合句由一个**主句**和一个或多个**从句**构成。从句虽然有自己的主谓结构，但不能独立成句，必须依附于主句。

### 1.1 从句分类

| 类型       | 功能                         | 相当于     |
| ---------- | ---------------------------- | ---------- |
| 名词性从句 | 充当主语、宾语、表语、同位语 | 一个名词   |
| 定语从句   | 修饰名词或代词               | 一个形容词 |
| 状语从句   | 修饰动词或整个句子           | 一个副词   |

## 2. 名词性从句 (Noun Clauses)

名词性从句包括**主语从句、宾语从句、表语从句、同位语从句**四种，其引导词相同：

| 引导词类型 | 引导词                        | 功能                     |
| ---------- | ----------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| 连接词     | that, whether, if             | 只起连接作用，不充当成分 |
| 连接代词   | who, whom, whose, what, which | 起连接+代词作用          |
| 连接副词   | when, where, why, how         | 起连接+副词作用          |

### 2.1 主语从句 (Subject Clause)

主语从句在复合句中充当主语：

> **That the earth is round** is a fact.
> **Whether she will come** remains uncertain.
> **What he said** surprised everyone.

**it 作形式主语：**

| 结构                | 示例                                            |
| ------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| It is + adj. + that | It is obvious **that she is right**.            |
| It is + n. + that   | It is a pity **that he left**.                  |
| It is + p.p. + that | It is reported **that the economy is growing**. |
| It + vi. + that     | It seems **that she is tired**.                 |

### 2.2 宾语从句 (Object Clause)

宾语从句在复合句中充当宾语：

> I know **that she is a programmer**.
> She asked **whether the code compiles**.
> I wonder **what he is doing**.

**宾语从句的时态呼应：**

| 主句时态 | 从句时态           | 示例                                  |
| -------- | ------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| 现在时   | 任意时态           | She says she **is/was/will be** busy. |
| 过去时   | 过去某时态         | She said she **was/would be** busy.   |
| 过去时   | 客观真理仍用现在时 | He said the earth **is** round.       |

**宾语从句的否定转移：**

> I **don't think** she is right. = I think she is not right.

think, believe, suppose, expect 等动词的否定通常转移到主句。

### 2.3 表语从句 (Predicative Clause)

表语从句在复合句中充当表语：

> The question is **whether we should continue**.
> That is **what I want to say**.
> The reason is **that the system crashed**.

**注意：** 表语从句中表原因时用 that，不用 because：

> The reason is **that** he was ill.
> The reason is **because** he was ill.

### 2.4 同位语从句 (Appositive Clause)

同位语从句解释说明前面的名词：

> The fact **that she passed the exam** delighted everyone.
> I have no idea **when he will arrive**.
> The question **whether to go** remains undecided.

**同位语从句 vs. 定语从句：**

| 区别          | 同位语从句         | 定语从句           |
| ------------- | ------------------ | ------------------ |
| that 的功能   | 连接词，不充当成分 | 关系代词，充当成分 |
| that 可否省略 | 不可               | 宾语时可省         |
| 内容关系      | 解释名词内容       | 修饰名词特征       |

> 同位语从句：The news **that he won** is exciting. （他赢了的消息——解释 news 的内容）
> 定语从句：The news **that he told me** is exciting. （他告诉我的消息——修饰 news 的特征）

## 3. 定语从句 (Attributive Clause)

### 3.1 关系词

| 类型     | 关系词 | 先行词 | 从句中充当 | 示例                             |
| -------- | ------ | ------ | ---------- | -------------------------------- |
| 关系代词 | who    | 人     | 主/宾      | The man **who** called you       |
|          | whom   | 人     | 宾         | The man **whom** I met           |
|          | whose  | 人/物  | 定语       | The man **whose** car was stolen |
|          | which  | 物     | 主/宾      | The book **which** I bought      |
|          | that   | 人/物  | 主/宾      | The book **that** I bought       |
| 关系副词 | when   | 时间   | 状语       | The day **when** we met          |
|          | where  | 地点   | 状语       | The place **where** we work      |
|          | why    | 原因   | 状语       | The reason **why** she left      |

### 3.2 限制性定语从句 vs. 非限制性定语从句

| 区别 | 限制性         | 非限制性 |
| ---- | -------------- | -------- |
| 标点 | 无逗号         | 有逗号   |
| 功能 | 必不可少的限定 | 补充说明 |
| that | 可用           | 不可用   |
| 省略 | 宾语可省       | 不可省   |

> 限制性：Students **who work hard** will succeed. （努力的学生会成功。）
> 非限制性：My father**, who is 60**, still works. （我父亲，60岁了，仍在工作。）

### 3.3 只能用 that 的情况

| 情况                                 | 示例                                           |
| ------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------- |
| 先行词是 all, everything, nothing 等 | Everything **that** exists takes up space.     |
| 先行词被最高级修饰                   | This is the best book **that** I've read.      |
| 先行词被序数词修饰                   | The first thing **that** I should do is study. |
| 先行词被 the only, the very 修饰     | She is the only person **that** understands.   |
| 先行词既有人又有物                   | The man and the horse **that** appeared        |

### 3.4 介词 + 关系代词

> The house **in which** he lives is old. = The house **which** he lives **in** is old.
> The reason **for which** she left is unknown.

### 3.5 as 引导的定语从句

| 用法                  | 示例                                           |
| --------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| 与 such/the same 连用 | I have never seen **such** a man **as** he is. |
| 非限制性定语从句      | **As** is known to all, the earth is round.    |

## 4. 状语从句 (Adverbial Clause)

### 4.1 时间状语从句

| 引导词           | 含义             | 示例                                           |
| ---------------- | ---------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| when             | 当…时            | **When** she arrived, we started.              |
| while            | 在…期间          | **While** she was reading, he was coding.      |
| as               | 一边…一边 / 随着 | **As** time passed, things changed.            |
| before           | 在…之前          | Think twice **before** you act.                |
| after            | 在…之后          | **After** she finished, she left.              |
| since            | 自从             | I have lived here **since** I was born.        |
| until/till       | 直到             | Wait **until** she returns.                    |
| as soon as       | 一…就            | **As soon as** she arrived, we started.        |
| hardly...when    | 刚…就            | **Hardly had** she arrived **when** it rained. |
| no sooner...than | 刚…就            | **No sooner had** she left **than** it rained. |

**时态要点：**

- 主句将来时 → 从句用现在时表将来：I'll call you **when I arrive**.
- since 从句用一般过去时，主句用现在完成时：I **have lived** here **since** I **was** born.

### 4.2 地点状语从句

| 引导词   | 示例                                       |
| -------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| where    | **Where** there is a will, there is a way. |
| wherever | **Wherever** you go, I'll follow.          |

### 4.3 原因状语从句

| 引导词  | 含义             | 示例                                           |
| ------- | ---------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| because | 因为（直接原因） | She stayed **because** she was ill.            |
| since   | 既然（已知原因） | **Since** you're here, let's start.            |
| as      | 由于             | **As** it was late, we went home.              |
| for     | 因为（补充说明） | It must be raining, **for** the ground is wet. |

**because vs. since vs. as vs. for：**

| 区别 | because      | since/as     | for          |
| ---- | ------------ | ------------ | ------------ |
| 位置 | 主句前后均可 | 通常在主句前 | 只能在主句后 |
| 语气 | 最强         | 较弱         | 最弱         |
| 功能 | 回答 why     | 提供背景     | 补充说明     |

### 4.4 目的状语从句

| 引导词        | 示例                                                       |
| ------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| so that       | She studied hard **so that** she could pass.               |
| in order that | **In order that** everyone may hear, speak loudly.         |
| for fear that | She locked the door **for fear that** someone might enter. |

**注意：** 目的状语从句中常含情态动词 can/could/may/might/will/would。

### 4.5 结果状语从句

| 引导词      | 示例                                                  |
| ----------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| so...that   | She was **so** tired **that** she fell asleep.        |
| such...that | It was **such** a good book **that** I read it twice. |
| so that     | She studied hard, **so that** she passed.             |

**so...that vs. such...that：**

$$\text{so} + \text{adj./adv.} + \text{that}$$
$$\text{such} + (a/an) + \text{adj.} + \text{n.} + \text{that}$$

> so beautiful a girl = such a beautiful girl

### 4.6 条件状语从句

| 引导词             | 含义            | 示例                                      |
| ------------------ | --------------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| if                 | 如果            | **If** it rains, I'll stay home.          |
| unless             | 除非 (= if not) | I won't go **unless** you go.             |
| provided/providing | 假如            | **Provided** you study, you'll pass.      |
| suppose/supposing  | 假设            | **Suppose** it rains, what shall we do?   |
| as long as         | 只要            | **As long as** you try, you'll succeed.   |
| on condition that  | 条件是          | I'll go **on condition that** you go too. |

**时态要点：** 主句将来时 → 从句用现在时表将来（主将从现）。

### 4.7 让步状语从句

| 引导词              | 示例                                          |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------- |
| although/though     | **Although** she was tired, she continued.    |
| even though/even if | **Even if** it rains, I'll go.                |
| while               | **While** I understand, I disagree.           |
| as                  | Cold **as** it was, she went out.             |
| whether...or        | **Whether** you agree **or** not, I'll do it. |
| no matter + wh-     | **No matter what** happens, stay calm.        |

**注意：** although/though 不能与 but 连用，但可以与 yet/still 连用。

### 4.8 方式状语从句

| 引导词          | 示例                                  |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| as              | Do **as** I say.                      |
| as if/as though | She acts **as if** she were the boss. |
| the way         | She cooks **the way** her mother did. |

**as if/as though 的虚拟语气：**

> 与现在相反：She acts as if she **were** the boss.
> 与过去相反：She looks as if she **had seen** a ghost.

### 4.9 比较状语从句

| 结构                       | 示例                                              |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| as...as                    | She is **as** tall **as** her sister.             |
| not so/as...as             | He is **not so** tall **as** his brother.         |
| 比较级 + than              | She is **taller than** her sister.                |
| the + 比较级, the + 比较级 | **The harder** you work, **the luckier** you get. |

## 5. 从句综合运用

### 5.1 从句嵌套

英语中从句可以嵌套使用，形成复杂句式：

> The fact **that the research **which was conducted last year **showed **that the drug is effective\*\* has changed the policy.

分析：

- 主句：The fact has changed the policy.
- 同位语从句：that the research showed that the drug is effective
- 定语从句：which was conducted last year（修饰 research）
- 宾语从句：that the drug is effective（作 showed 的宾语）

### 5.2 从句选择要点

| 需要表达       | 选用从句                   |
| -------------- | -------------------------- |
| 事物内容/事实  | 名词性从句 (that)          |
| 人/物的特征    | 定语从句 (who/which/that)  |
| 时间/条件/原因 | 状语从句 (when/if/because) |
| 是否/选择      | 名词性从句 (whether)       |
| 疑问内容       | 名词性从句 (wh-词)         |
